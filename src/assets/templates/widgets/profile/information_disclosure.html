UC Berkeley defines directory information as personal information such as your address, email, name, phone, and academic standing.

You may restrict some or all of your information from being treated as directory information by managing your FERPA restrictions.

To learn more about how UC Berkeley protects the privacy of student records, visit the FERPA Privacy Disclosure page.

Public directory information can be updated at the campus online directory. Make changes to contact information used for official campus communications using the links to the left.
